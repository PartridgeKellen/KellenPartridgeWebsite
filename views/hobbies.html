Hiking and walking

My favorite activity is to go on hikes and walks. I find this to be the best way to clear my head, as well as get some exercise while I'm at it. It also happens to be the best way to see beautiful scenery, especially in Colorado.

Playing video games

I grew up playing lots and lots of video games: Halo, Elder Scrolls, Battlefield, and more. Though I don't have the amount of freetime that I used to, I find that games are a great way to have fun and connect with friends.

Eating new foods

Trying new foods is not only a great way to 'spice' up life, but it also happens to be a great way to learn about other cultures, create new experiences with friends, and create reasons to go to new places.
